Full modded server with SAPP

Installation:
Don't forget to make backup from your original files!

Halo CE only!

Copy the files from the "Halo CE" folder to the Halo CE main folder, they are usually in the "Program Files" or "Program Files(x86)" folder.
Copy the "My Games" folder to your Documents folder.
You can customise your server by editing the init.txt and other files.
Now start your server and Have Fun. ^^
